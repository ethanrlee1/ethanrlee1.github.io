What is Tae Kwon Do?

Tae Kwon Do is a Korean martial art style for self-defense. It emphasizes fast strikes utlizing kicks, jumps, and forms. It requires self control, discipline, kindness and humility.

Ballantyne Kicks is a taekwondo school that teaches self-defense, it also houses brazilian jiu jitsu.

About Ballantyne Kicks

Ballanytne Kicks is a taekwondo school located in North Carolina. There are instructor, most notably Master Vincent and Grand Master Russell. They teach over 15 forms of the style. There are over 7 belt colors, black being the last belt. Afterward in which several tiers of black belts can be achieved with 4th degree being master level.

Taekwondo is a way to get exercise in. Ballantyne Kicks also emphasizes discipline at home.

Best Times

How It Works

In my experince we have taekwondo instructors teach techniques and forms to the students. After learning and mastering them, they are put through a test before moving up the next belt color. Tests and techniques get more advanced and difficult as you move to the next belt color.

When I was practicing for my tests, I always practiced at home and watched a couple youtube videos to help me.

Why I Did It?

When I was younger, my parents had me try out multiple different sports. None of them stuck with me, till I tried taekwondo. I always thought the reason my dad registered me for it was that I needed self-defense as I was bullied alot in elementary. After a decade of practicing, I can condfidently say it was the right sport for me.

I highly reccomend it for anyone as an option for a sport. It is fun and stress reliving.
