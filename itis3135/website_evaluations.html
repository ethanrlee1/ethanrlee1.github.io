Website Evaluations

Good Website: https://www.homedepot.com/

Home Depot's website is used to shop and puruse for materials at Home Depot

  • Contrast: Basic colors used thematically and matches the theme of Home Depot.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, and colors throughout the website.
  • Alignment: Elements are well-aligned, creating a balanced and structured look.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together, improving readability and usability.

Bad Website: https://www.art.yale.edu/

Yale School of Art website was desgined to show how bad a website can be

  • Contrast: Colors clash with each other and too different.
  • Repetition: No consistent colors font style.
  • Alignment: No alignment on text and images, all random.
  • Proximity: Overcrowded content with little space between elements and placed randomly.
