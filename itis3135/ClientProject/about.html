About TEC Club

TEC Club is a welcoming social club for students who enjoy technology, creativity, and community. While we have a strong focus on computer science and tech, all students are invited to join and participate in our fun and engaging activities.

From movie nights and gaming sessions to creative art showcases and collaborative projects, TEC Club provides a space to connect, relax, and explore interests with fellow students. Whether you're into coding, drawing, or just want to meet like-minded peers, TEC Club has something for you!
